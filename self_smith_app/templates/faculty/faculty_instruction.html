Read the Instructions Carefully!

The game consists of questions carefully designed to help induct you as an academician in a technical education course. The objective of the game is to learn. Not winning or losing. Each question in the quiz is of a multiple-choice format. Read each question carefully, and click on the button next to your response. The questions are curated according to the level of difficulty and there are 4 levels.

The content of each level is of 6 categories: Innovative Pedagogies, Student Psychology, Communication Skills, Course & Programme Outcome, Ethics & Values and Research Culture. Each correct or incorrect response will result in appropriate feedback immediately at the bottom of the screen.

After responding to a question, click on the "Next Question" button at the bottom to go to the next question. After responding to the last question, click on "Close" on the top of the window to exit the quiz.
